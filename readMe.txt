

   This program works with movements like A 1, B 7, C 10 and works with commands like "SAVE FILE.TXT" , "LOAD FILE.TXT" , " -- UNDO " or " UNDO -- "
   that the user will enter.

   
   When the 2 hex game was over, I printed the total number of marked cells in these two games and the comparison of the number of marked cells in both games to screen.

   
   My logic to show the users' score is as follows. 
   To increase User1's score, it is necessary to link with a 'x' cell from the leftmost column of the board.
   
   To increase User2's or computer's score, it is necessary to link with a 'o' cell from the top row of the board.
